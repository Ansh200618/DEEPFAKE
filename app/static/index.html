How It Works

🔬

Error Level Analysis

Re-saves the image at a known JPEG quality and computes pixel-level differences. Manipulated regions show higher error levels than authentic areas.

📡

Frequency Fingerprinting

GAN-generated content leaves characteristic signatures in the DCT domain. DeepGuard computes high-frequency energy ratios to expose synthetic generation artefacts.

🎚️

MFCC Voice Analysis

Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients, pitch trajectory, and spectral flatness are extracted from audio. Voice-cloned speech exhibits unnaturally smooth MFCC variance.

🧠

NLP Credibility Scoring

Clickbait patterns, emotional manipulation language, factual density, and structural red-flags are combined to produce a fake-news probability score.

🎞️

Temporal Video Analysis

Optical-flow analysis detects face-swap boundary artefacts. Blink-rate and face-presence consistency are evaluated across sampled frames.

⚖️

Weighted Score Fusion

All sub-scores are fused into a single interpretable confidence value with FAKE / SUSPICIOUS / REAL classification and human-readable flags.

About DeepGuard

DeepGuard is an open-source research platform designed to combat AI-generated misinformation. As synthetic media becomes indistinguishable from authentic content, robust forensic tooling becomes essential for journalists, law-enforcement, and everyday users.

The platform operates entirely locally — no content is uploaded to third-party servers — ensuring privacy and making it suitable for sensitive investigations.

  • ✅ Free and open-source (MIT licence)
  • ✅ No cloud dependency — runs 100 % on-device
  • ✅ REST API for integration into other tools
  • ✅ Installable as a system service on Linux
  • ✅ Extensible detector pipeline
